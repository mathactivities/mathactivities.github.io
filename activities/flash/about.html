What is Gorrrilla Monk

Gorrrila Monk is dedicated to provide innocent young children happiness when all of their freedom is pulled away by their schools. We want to give students a new modern-looking platform to game. This is a website created only by one creator who is Krupal Tisgaonkar (12-years-old). He is fascinated by technology and biology, starting off their life with a simple platform to game.
